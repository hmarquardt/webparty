Browser Party Line is a small-room WebRTC mesh party line. GitHub Pages hosts this page, and a Cloudflare Worker with a Durable Object only coordinates room signaling.
Video and audio usually flow browser-to-browser. TURN may relay media when direct peer-to-peer connection fails, which costs bandwidth money.
Mesh does not scale well. Two people is easy. Three to four people is the intended range. Five to six may work but can get janky. Seven or more should use an SFU such as LiveKit, Jitsi, Janus, mediasoup, or Daily.
There are no accounts, no moderation system, no room passwords, and no authentication in v3. Anyone with the room link can join unless future access control is added.
